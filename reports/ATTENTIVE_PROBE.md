# Layer 2 — Attentive MLP Probe (AD vs CN)

Frozen Neuro-JEPA embedding → nonlinear MLP head vs linear probe, both run through
the same leakage-honest machinery (StandardScaler + in-fold auto-PCA, site-disjoint
CV, bootstrap 95% CI, permutation null, repeated-CV ensembling). Generated by
`scripts/run_attentive_probe.py` → `reports/attentive_probe_ad.json`.

**Paper reference.** NeuroVFM (Nat Med 2026, *Health system learning enables
generalist neuroimaging models*, s41591-026-04497-1) freezes a Vol-JEPA encoder
trained on **5.24M** clinical volumes and trains **attentive MLP probes** for
downstream diagnosis. We adapt honestly to a **pooled 768-d** embedding at **n in
the hundreds**: we *report* whether the nonlinear head helps rather than assume it,
and substitute leave-one-group-out (LOGO) attribution for the spatial attention
maps a pooled embedding cannot provide.

## Results

| Cohort | n (AD/CN) | Linear AUC [95% CI] | MLP AUC [95% CI] | Δ (MLP−linear) | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ADNI | 590 (87/503) | **0.859** [0.817, 0.898] | 0.857 [0.814, 0.899] | **−0.0015** | MLP *matches* linear — embedding already near-linearly separable |
| OASIS-1 | 164 (45/119) | **0.730** [0.634, 0.813] | 0.661 [0.565, 0.743] | **−0.068** | MLP *does not beat* linear at this n |
| OASIS-2 | 98 (13/85) | **0.757** [0.577, 0.888] | 0.735 [0.554, 0.875] | **−0.022** | MLP *does not beat* linear at this n |

All permutation p ≤ 0.006 (signal is real); the nonlinear head simply buys nothing
over the linear one. **Headline classification is delivered by the linear probe.**

## Interpretable grounding (LOGO AUC drop)

Larger drop ⇒ that block carries more of the AD-vs-CN signal.

- **ADNI** (full AUC 0.862): top driver **p_tau217** (+0.0027); gfap +0.0004,
  nfl +0.0002, age 0.0. The **embedding is redundant given plasma** here
  (drop −0.051) — plasma p-tau217 already carries the diagnostic signal.
- **OASIS-1** (full AUC 0.729): top driver **embedding** (+0.142) — no plasma
  markers in OASIS, so structure via the frozen embedding is the whole signal.
- **OASIS-2** (full AUC 0.753): top driver **embedding** (+0.425).

**Takeaway.** Where plasma exists (ADNI) it dominates and the image embedding is
redundant; where it doesn't (OASIS) the frozen embedding *is* the signal. This is
the honest "what drives the signal" story the pipeline diagram promised for
Layer 2 — and it explains why the nonlinear MLP head adds nothing: at these sample
sizes the frozen foundation-model embedding is already linearly separable.
